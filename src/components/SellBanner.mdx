# SellBanner

A full-width call-to-action section that prompts users to sell their car. Renders a dark background
with a headline, supporting text, and a "Sell My Car" button.

## Usage

<Canvas of={SellBannerStories.Default} />
<Controls of={SellBannerStories.Default} />

## Props

| Prop | Type | Default | Description |
|------|------|---------|-------------|
| `onSellClick` | `() => void` | `undefined` | Called when the "Sell My Car" button is clicked. |

## Design notes

- The background colour `#0f172a` matches the `AppFooter` dark surface — they are typically placed
  adjacent on the page to form a visual anchor at the bottom.
- The component is rendered as a `<Box component="section">` for semantic HTML.
- Copy ("Ready to Sell Your Car?", "Get a fair valuation…") is currently hardcoded. Extract to
  props when the component needs to be reused with different messaging.
